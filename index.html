ABOUT WOLVESVILLE SIMULATION

Key features

Gameplay

A social deduction multiplayer game having different teams fighting to be the last one standing!

Roles

Use special abilities to uncover the roles of other players and convince your fellow players to work with you.

Perks

Along with Discord perks, Quests to complete challenges daily. Ranked, Sandbox, Custom gamemodes to enjoy fullest!

Community

Many Events, exciting mini games, prizewinning giveaways, brain-boosting puzzles alongwith friendly and active community.

THE TEAMS

The building blocks of this game

Team Village

Seer, doctor, jailer, forger, gunner like roles which help sustaining village. They hunt for evils.

Team Werewolves

The evil roles as Werewolf, Alpha, Berserk etc. who are hidden in village. They aim to conquere village.

Team Solo

The solo roles such as Arsonist, Fool, Bomber, Serial Killer which win by themselves with their deeds.

Team Cupid

In the beginning of the game, two people are coupled. They win as Cupid iff couple is alive at the end.
